Choose a date and time below to register for a workshop.

I promise it will be worth your time and I will show exactly how to save and pay for college to ANY school in the country and get a full refund on your money during retirement using a strategy I call “The Retirement Scholarship Strategy.”

This strategy works for any school no matter how much it costs.

This strategy works for those with younger students or those who already have kids in high school or college.

This strategy works for those who have nothing saved for college or those who have enough saved to pay for college.

You will learn why you shouldn’t use your 529 accounts or savings accounts to pay for college.

You will learn why you shouldn’t pay for college out of your pocket with your monthly cashflow.

You will learn why you shouldn’t use private loans to pay for college.

You will learn why you shouldn’t use a home equity loan to pay for college.

You will learn why you shouldn’t use whole life insurance to save or pay for college.

And so much more!
